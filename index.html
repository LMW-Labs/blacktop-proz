Sealcoating

The $300 Service That Saves You $5,000

Here's the secret most homeowners don't know: unsealed asphalt fails in 7-10 years. Properly sealcoated asphalt lasts 20-25 years. That's not marketing—that's chemistry. UV rays oxidize asphalt binder, turning it gray and brittle. Sealcoat blocks the sun, repels water, and keeps your pavement flexible.

Professional sealcoating should be done every 2-3 years. It's a fraction of the cost of replacement and makes your driveway look brand new overnight. We use commercial-grade coal tar or asphalt emulsion sealers—not the watered-down stuff from the hardware store.

Sealcoating Benefits:

  • ✓ Extends pavement life by 3x (from 8 years to 25+)
  • ✓ Protects against UV damage and oxidation
  • ✓ Repels water, oil, and gas spills
  • ✓ Restores rich black appearance instantly
  • ✓ Fills minor surface cracks and imperfections
  • ✓ Costs 1/10th the price of replacement

Is your driveway gray instead of black? It's begging to be sealed before winter.
